# Transaction Velocity Rules

## 1-Hour Velocity Alert
More than 5 transactions from the same account within any rolling 1-hour window triggers an automatic velocity alert. The compliance system must log the event and flag all transactions in that window for review.

## 24-Hour Velocity Review
More than 20 transactions from a single account within a 24-hour period requires mandatory manual review regardless of individual transaction amounts. The account is placed in a monitoring queue until a compliance officer clears it.

## Rapid Succession Transfers
Three or more outbound wire transfers to different beneficiaries within 2 hours is classified as a rapid succession pattern. This requires immediate escalation to the fraud team regardless of amounts.

## Daily Debit Limits
Accounts with a risk score above 0.7 are subject to enhanced velocity monitoring:
- More than 3 transactions over $1,000 within 1 hour triggers review
- More than 10 debit transactions in 24 hours triggers review

## Card-Not-Present Velocity
More than 3 declined card-not-present transactions within 30 minutes must be escalated to the fraud prevention team and may result in temporary card suspension pending customer verification.
